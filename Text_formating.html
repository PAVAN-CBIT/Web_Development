Explained: How Lionel Messi pipped goal-machine Robert Lewandowski to Ballon d’Or

After the 2020 Ballon d’Or awards were cancelled due to the pandemic, it was felt that Robert Lewandowski would make up for the lost opportunity this year. But it wasn’t to be as Lionel Messi took the crown ahead of the Bayern Munich striker followed by Chelsea and Italy midfielder Jorginho.There are three basic criteria that the selection panel is looking at. First, individual as well as collective performances. These focus on titles won with their respective club and/or country. Secondly, the jury focuses on the talent of the player and whether that player is a proponent of fair play in the world of football.
